About Me

I am currently a Postdoc Associate at MMVC Lab at New York University (NYU) and Center for AI and Robotics (CAIR) at NYU Abu Dhabi (NYUAD). Before that, I received my Bachelorâ€™s degree in Geographic Information System from China University of Petroleum in 2016 and the Ph.D. degree in Cartography and Geographic Information System from the Aerospace Information Research Institute, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2021. During my PhD year, I have been fortunate to be a visiting scholar at AI4CE Lab at New York University (NYU).

My research interests lie in the intersection of GIS, Remote Sensing, 3D Computer Vision and 3D Deep learning, which is aimed to empoly Artificial Intelligence to improve GIS and Remote Sensing. Specifically, my research interests include: 3D Shape Represtation Learning, 3D Secne Understanding, 3D Reconstruction and Smart City Applications.
